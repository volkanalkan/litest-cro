CLINICAL OPERATIONS & PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Clinical research projects largely rely on Clinical Research Site Coordinators (SCs) who serve as the primary link between researchers and volunteers. They have a wide range of responsibilities including enrolling participants, providing specialized care related to the study, monitoring progress, collecting data, processing samples, and conducting follow-ups.

FEASIBILITY ACTIVITIES AND SITE SELECTION

The selection of the right site and investigators is crucial for the success of a study. Based on therapeutic area expertise, the most qualified investigators are selected, and feasibility visits are conducted to gather information about participants' interest in participating in clinical trials.
