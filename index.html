Outsource Solutions

With its experienced and business-oriented team, Litest offers outsourcing services to support and facilitate the field management of clinical studies supported by international pharmaceutical companies. These services; Clinical Research Associate (CRA)- Center/Study Coordinator (SC)- Study Nurse (SN)

General Qualifications Required in the Position Seeking

We are looking for an experienced Center/Study Coordinator who will ensure that drug studies are carried out in clinical research centers in accordance with the Good Clinical Practice Guide (IKU/ICH-GCP) and Regulations, with the supervision and supervision of responsible investigators.

Desired Qualifications

We are seeking graduates with backgrounds in health and science (e.g., Biology, Chemistry, Biochemistry, Molecular Biology, Genetics) proficient in MS Office, English reading, and writing. Candidates should be dedicated to medical documentation and reporting processes, with no travel restrictions. Ideal teammates possess strong interpersonal skills, representation abilities, and are interested in part-time or full-time positions in clinical research and the pharmaceutical industry.

About Us

LITEST group of companies is a Contract Research Organization and the first Turkish company to produce Bioresonance Based Allergy tests and food intolerance tests. LITEST is a company that is a leader in understanding and meeting the needs of global sponsors, succeeds in clinical research with its extensive network of researchers and knowledge of local legislation, and manages to overcome the challenges of every clinical study it manages. As LITEST, our main goal in clinical research management is to provide all kinds of services related to clinical research to sponsor companies, research centers and responsible study operators in line with their needs.
