Job Title & Job Description

Clinical Research Field Representatives are sought for international pharmaceutical companies' clinical trials, to support physicians (throughout Turkey) on a Full-time / Part-time basis.

General Qualifications

We are looking for team members who can contribute effectively:

● Graduates of Universities with degrees in Physics, Chemistry, Science, Biology, and Health Departments
● Proficient in English at least at B2 level
● Able to use MS Office applications
● Strong communication skills
● Articulate
● Solution-oriented
● Team player with good follow-up skills

Job Description

Support physicians by visiting designated hospitals for the company's clinical trials, ensuring adherence to current regulations, protocols, and other relevant documents from start to finish, and reporting back to the company.
